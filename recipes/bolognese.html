Bolognese Pasta

Description

This is a delicous recipe for bolognese pasta. It is very simple to make, with the prep time lasting around 15 minutes. Forget about going to your favorite restaurant for a good italian meal. Instead, create this masterpiece in the comfort of your own home!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • ground beef
  • 1 carrot
  • 1/4 cup red wine
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 can peeled tomatoes
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • pasta
  • salt
  • basil
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Heat skillet over medium-high heat with olive oil
  2. Saute garlic and onion until onion is translucent
  3. Shred the carrot
  4. Turn heat down to low to keep a constant simmer
  5. Add the peeled and crushed tomatoes and carrot
  6. Mix in meat
  7. Add wine
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste
  9. Let cook for 35 minutes on low
  10. Make pasta, stain on high heat with basil and a small amount of sauce, then mix in the rest of the sauce.
